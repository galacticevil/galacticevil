# Project brief

## Purpose

Galactic Evil is the professional headquarters of Rory Cottle: senior software engineer, former CTO, fractional technical leader, architect, advisor, and project-rescue specialist.

The primary audience is US startups and product businesses seeking senior technical capability without immediately hiring a permanent executive. South African and other international clients are explicitly welcome. Selected permanent senior or leadership roles remain secondary.

## Positioning

Working headline:

> Technical leadership for ambitious software and unstable planetary conditions.

Working description:

> I'm Rory Cottle, a senior software engineer and former CTO helping startups and product teams design systems, guide engineering teams, rescue difficult projects, and ship dependable software.

International positioning:

> Based in South Africa and working remotely with companies in the United States and internationally.

Tone anchor:

> The name is dramatic. The engineering is not.

## Brand behaviour

The site should be capable first, distinctive second, and playful third. It presents a legitimate interstellar technical organisation with dry bureaucratic humour. References reward attention but never obstruct meaning.

The voice is direct, experienced, calm, curious, human, and lightly theatrical. Avoid aggressive villain language, juvenile jokes, generic consultancy phrases, and dense sci-fi jargon.

Humour is welcome in headings, microcopy, loading and empty states, the footer, the 404 page, and easter eggs. Restrain it in outcomes, testimonials, legal copy, technical explanations, CV material, and form confirmations.

## Core capabilities

1. Fractional CTO
2. Contract engineering
3. Architecture and advisory
4. Project rescue

Engagements may be fractional, contract, fixed-scope, retained, or recovery-focused. Do not publish rates initially.
